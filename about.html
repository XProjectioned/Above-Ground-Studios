About Me

Hi! My name is Shaun Cooper and I am a photographer. Many people compare photography to painting, but quite frankly, I despise painting. Thats why I see photography in a different way. I see it as a science, the precision of capturing a moment in time. That's why I love the technical aspects of photography, composition, lighting, and understanding my equipment. These elements are what make photography so amazing.
